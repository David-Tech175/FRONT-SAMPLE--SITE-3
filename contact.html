Contact

Contact FVAP at the following:

Email

vote@fvap.gov

Media Inquiries

media@fvap.gov

International Phone and Fax Numbers

Toll-Free International Phone and Fax Numbers

Telephone

  • Toll-free: 1-800-438-VOTE (8683)
  • DSN: 425-1584

Support Hours of Operation: 7:30 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. ET, Monday-Friday excluding Federal Holidays.

Mail*

Federal Voting Assistance Program
Department of Defense
4800 Mark Center Drive, Suite 05E22 Alexandria, VA 22350-5000

* Do not send registration and absentee ballot request forms to this address. Refer to "Find Out Where to Send Election Materials".

Veterans/Military Crisis Line

  • CONUS: Call 988 and Press 1 or go to https://www.veteranscrisisline.net/ 
  • In Europe: 844-702-5495 or DSN 988
  • In the Pacific: 844-702-5493 or DSN 988
  • In Southwest Asia: 855-422-7719 or DSN 988
  • Chat: Text with a crisis line responder. Send a text to 838255 or through https://www.veteranscrisisline.net/chat
